Networks of the University.

Physical expressions of the connected infrastructure inside the school environment.

Ethernet Connection to Computer

This ethernet cable provides a slightly different role to the one at home. It allows for the networking of all the media lab computers. This allows for any student to sit at any computer and access their personal account and files

Networked Computers

All computers in the lab allow students to access all thier files. They can also offload highly compute intensive tasks, such as rendering complex 3D scenes to a render farm on the network, allowing them to take advantage of the collective computing power of many more GPU's.
