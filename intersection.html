Networks of the Intersection.

Physical expressions of internet infrasstructure in a regular street environment.

Traffic Control Box

Present on all traffic lights, this box seems to be intergral to the traffic system running smoothly.

Larger Networking Box on Traffic Light

This box was present on only a select few traffic lights, and seemed signifigantly larger than the smaller metal boxes above. There was usually one of these larger boxes at each intersection, rather than one per light.

Traffic Light Cap Piece

All traffic lights seem to feed all cables into this top cap piece. This could be a way to provide a weather sealed place to have wire connections to the control system.

TelstraSaturn operated manhole cover

These are the largest circular covers present in Wellington - often with multiple near each other. They are one of the largest companies responsible for maintaining physical infrasture in Wellington alongside Downer Connect.

Telecom operated manhole cover

These covers often were on sidewalks, rather than in streets like the Testra ones above. They are aso much larger, and hence made of concrete rather than metal. They would now be operated by Spark, now that Telecom has closed.
